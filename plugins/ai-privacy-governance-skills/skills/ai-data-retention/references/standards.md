# Standards — AI Data Retention and Unlearning

## Primary Legislation
- **GDPR Art. 5(1)(e)**: Storage limitation — data kept no longer than necessary.
- **GDPR Art. 17**: Right to erasure — applicable to training data and model artefacts encoding personal data.
- **GDPR Art. 25(2)**: Data protection by default — only data necessary for purpose processed and retained.
- **AI Act Art. 12**: Record-keeping obligations for high-risk AI.
- **AI Act Art. 10**: Data governance including data lifecycle management.

## EDPB Guidance
- **EDPB Guidelines 04/2025**: Training data retention justification; model encoding of personal data.
- **EDPB ChatGPT Taskforce (2024)**: Erasure capability for training data.
