# NIST PF COMMUNICATE Function — Standards References

## Primary Standards

### NIST Privacy Framework Version 1.0
- **Published**: January 16, 2020
- **Relevance**: Defines COMMUNICATE function subcategories CM.AW and CM.PO

### Article 29 Working Party WP260 — Guidelines on Transparency
- **Published**: November 2017 (revised April 2018)
- **Relevance**: Detailed guidance on privacy notice content, format, and delivery

### ISO/IEC 29184:2020 — Online Privacy Notices and Consent
- **Relevance**: Standard for online privacy notice structure and consent collection

### EDPB Guidelines 04/2022 — Calculation of Administrative Fines
- **Relevance**: Enforcement context for transparency failures
