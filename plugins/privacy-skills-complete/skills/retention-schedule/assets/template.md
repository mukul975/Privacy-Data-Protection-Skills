## Annual Review Checklist

- [ ] Legislative scan completed (new/amended statutes)
- [ ] Regulatory guidance review (ICO, EDPB, sector-specific)
- [ ] All data categories reviewed for purpose validity
- [ ] Retention periods confirmed or updated
- [ ] Technical enforcement verified (automated deletion active)
- [ ] Privacy notice updated (if periods changed)
- [ ] ROPA updated (Art. 30(1)(f))
- [ ] Board reporting metrics prepared
- [ ] Training materials updated
- [ ] DPO sign-off obtained
