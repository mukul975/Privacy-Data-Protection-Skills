### §2.33 TPO Consent Implementation
- [ ] §2.33 TPO consent form developed and reviewed by legal counsel

### Traditional §2.31 Consent
- [ ] Consent form contains all 10 required elements
- [ ] Consent tracking system in place (active/expired/revoked)
