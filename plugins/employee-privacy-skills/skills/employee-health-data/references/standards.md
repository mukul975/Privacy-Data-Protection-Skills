# Standards and Regulatory References — Employee Health Data

## Primary Legislation

### GDPR
- Art. 4(15): Definition of data concerning health.
- Art. 9(1): General prohibition on processing health data.
- Art. 9(2)(a): Explicit consent — rarely valid in employment.
- Art. 9(2)(b): Employment law obligations — primary basis for occupational health.
- Art. 9(2)(h): Health professional processing — for occupational medicine.
- Art. 9(2)(i): Public health — pandemic response.
- Art. 9(3): Health data processed by professionals bound by secrecy.

### EU Directives
- Framework Directive 89/391/EEC: Employer obligations for health and safety.
- Working Time Directive 2003/88/EC: Working hour limits affecting fitness assessment.

### National Implementations
- **UK**: DPA 2018, Schedule 1, Part 1, Para 1; Equality Act 2010; Access to Medical Reports Act 1988.
- **Germany**: BDSG Section 26(3); ArbSchG (Occupational Safety Act).
- **France**: Labour Code Art. L.4624-1 et seq.; occupational health surveillance framework.
- **Italy**: D.Lgs. 81/2008 Art. 25, 41; competent doctor (medico competente) system.
- **Netherlands**: UAVG Art. 30(1)(a); Arbeidsomstandighedenwet (Working Conditions Act).
