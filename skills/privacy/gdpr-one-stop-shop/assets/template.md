# Lead Supervisory Authority Determination -- Nexus Technologies GmbH

**Assessment Date**: 2026-01-20
**Assessor**: Dr. Katharina Weiss, DPO

---

## Establishment Analysis

| Establishment | Country | Function | Processing Decisions |
|--------------|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Nexus Technologies GmbH (HQ) | Germany (Berlin) | Central administration, product development, all strategic decisions | Yes -- all processing purposes and means determined here |
| Nexus Technologies Ireland Ltd | Ireland (Dublin) | EU sales office, customer support | No -- follows Berlin instructions |
| Nexus Technologies France SAS | France (Paris) | Marketing and business development | No -- follows Berlin instructions |

## Determination

- **Entity type**: Controller
- **Central administration**: Germany (Berlin)
- **Processing decision location**: Germany (Berlin) -- same as central administration
- **Main establishment**: Germany (Berlin) per Art. 4(16)(a)
- **Lead supervisory authority**: Bundesbeauftragter fuer den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI)
- **Concerned authorities**: Irish DPC (for Ireland establishment), CNIL (for France establishment)

## Practical Implications

1. BfDI is the primary regulatory contact for cross-border processing matters.
2. Complaints filed with CNIL or Irish DPC regarding cross-border processing will be referred to BfDI under Art. 56.
3. Nexus maintains cooperative relationships with all three authorities per Art. 31.
