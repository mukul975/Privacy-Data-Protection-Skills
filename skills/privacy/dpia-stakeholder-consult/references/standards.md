# Regulatory Standards -- DPIA Stakeholder Consultation

## Primary Legislation

### GDPR Article 35(9) -- Data Subject Views
"The controller shall, where appropriate, seek the views of data subjects or their representatives on the intended processing, without prejudice to the protection of commercial or public interests or the security of processing operations."

### GDPR Article 35(2) -- DPO Advice
"The controller shall seek the advice of the data protection officer, where designated, when carrying out a data protection impact assessment."

### GDPR Recital 84 -- Consultation Rationale
"In order to enhance compliance with this Regulation where processing operations are likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons, the controller should be responsible for the carrying-out of a data protection impact assessment."

### GDPR Article 36(1) -- Prior Consultation
Evidence of stakeholder consultation is a key element of any prior consultation submission to a supervisory authority.

### GDPR Article 36(3)(c) -- Prior Consultation Content
Prior consultation submission must include "the respective responsibilities of the controller, joint controllers and processors involved in the processing" including evidence of stakeholder engagement.

## Regulatory Guidance

### EDPB WP248rev.01 -- DPIA Guidelines (Section III.E)
- "It is good practice for the controller to seek the views of data subjects or their representatives"
- Controllers should document reasons where consultation is not pursued
- Consultation should occur early enough to genuinely influence the DPIA
- Multiple consultation methods may be combined for comprehensive coverage

### ICO DPIA Guidance -- Consultation Chapter (2023)
- Recommends consulting data subjects unless there is a "compelling reason" not to
- Provides examples of proportionate consultation methods
- Notes that "seeking the views" does not mean obtaining consent
- Emphasises that commercial confidentiality may limit but not eliminate consultation

### CNIL Guide on DPIA (2024)
- Recommends stakeholder mapping before consultation design
- Provides templates for consultation questionnaires
- Notes that employee representative bodies (works councils, CSE) must be consulted for employee monitoring DPIAs under French law

### BfDI Guidance on DPIA Stakeholder Engagement (2022)
- Works council (Betriebsrat) consultation is legally required under BetrVG for employee monitoring
- Patient advisory councils recommended for health data processing DPIAs
- Consumer association engagement recommended for large-scale consumer profiling

## Enforcement Context

### EDPS Decision on EU Institutions DPIA (Opinion 5/2019)
European Data Protection Supervisor emphasised that failure to consult data subjects during DPIA must be justified, and that "the views of data subjects shall be sought by the most appropriate means."

### Spanish AEPD Guidelines on DPIAs (2021)
Requires documentation of consultation process and explicit recording of how data subject views were addressed in the final DPIA.

### Dutch AP Guidance on DPIA Public Consultation
Recommends publication of DPIA summaries for public-sector processing to enable broader stakeholder input.
