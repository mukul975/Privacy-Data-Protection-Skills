## Workflow 2: Risk Scoring and Prioritization

```

‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Step 1: Determine Likelihood (1-5 scale)
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Threat source capability and motivation
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Vulnerability severity and exploitability
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Current control effectiveness
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Historical incident frequency

‚îÇ
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Step 2: Determine Impact (1-5 scale)
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Number of individuals potentially affected
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Sensitivity of ePHI at risk
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Operational impact (clinical care disruption)
‚îÇ   ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Financial impact (fines, remediation, litigation)
‚îÇ   ‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ Reputational impact
‚îÇ
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Step 3: Calculate Risk Score
‚îÇ   ‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ Risk = Likelihood √ó Impact
‚îÇ       ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ 20-25: Critical ‚Üí Immediate action required
‚îÇ       ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ 12-19: High ‚Üí Action within 30 days

‚îÇ       ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ 2-5: Low ‚Üí Monitor in normal operations
‚îÇ       ‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ 1: Minimal ‚Üí Accept and document

‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ Step 4: Risk Response Decision
    ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Mitigate: Implement controls to reduce risk
    ‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Transfer: Insurance, BA contractual allocation
```
