## Step 5: Decision and Recommendation

**Decision**: Helix Biotech Diagnostics S.A.S. must maintain a RoPA despite having fewer than 250 employees, because:

1. The organisation processes special category data (health data in diagnostic services and employee sick leave), meeting Exception Condition 3.
2. All core processing activities are non-occasional (monthly, weekly, daily, ongoing), meeting Exception Condition 2.
3. Processing of financial and health data creates risk to data subjects' rights, meeting Exception Condition 1.

**Recommendation**: Maintain a complete RoPA for all processing activities using the CNIL simplified template for small organisations. The one activity that might technically qualify for exemption (office relocation) should also be documented for accountability purposes and minimal additional effort.
