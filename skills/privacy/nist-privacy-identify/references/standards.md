# Standards and References

## NIST Privacy Framework Version 1.0 (January 2020)
- Core Functions: IDENTIFY, GOVERN, CONTROL, COMMUNICATE, PROTECT
- IDENTIFY subcategories: ID.IM, ID.BE, ID.RA, ID.DE
- Companion Roadmap: NIST PF Roadmap for Improving Critical Infrastructure Privacy

## NIST Crosswalk Documents
- NIST PF to GDPR Crosswalk (NIST, 2020)
- NIST PF to ISO/IEC 27701 Crosswalk
- NIST PF to NIST Cybersecurity Framework Crosswalk

## Related Standards
- **ISO/IEC 27701:2019**: Privacy information management. Maps to NIST PF IDENTIFY through PII inventory and risk assessment requirements.
- **ISO/IEC 29134:2023**: PIA guidelines. Aligned with ID.RA risk assessment.
- **ISO 31000:2018**: Risk management. Foundation for NIST PF risk approach.
