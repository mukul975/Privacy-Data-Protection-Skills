## Determination

**DPIA recommended** — 1 EDPB criterion met (vulnerable data subjects) plus 1 additional risk factor (new activity).

## DPO Decision

After reviewing the PTA, I determine that a full DPIA is not required for this processing activity because: (1) the survey is voluntary, (2) data is pseudonymised, (3) no health data is collected, (4) aggregate analytics only, (5) standard technology. However, the following conditions apply:

1. Survey responses must not be linked to patient clinical records.
2. Free-text comments must be reviewed and redacted for any health information before inclusion in the dashboard.
3. Data retention limited to 24 months with automated deletion.
4. PTA to be re-screened if the survey scope changes to include health outcomes or clinical data.
