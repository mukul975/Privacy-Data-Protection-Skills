# Standards and Regulatory References — AI Bias and Special Categories

## Primary Legislation

### GDPR
- **Art. 9**: Processing of special categories of personal data — prohibited except under specified conditions.
- **Art. 5(1)(a)**: Fairness principle — processing must be fair to data subjects.
- **Art. 22(4)**: Automated decisions based on Art. 9 data only under Art. 9(2)(a) or (g).
- **Art. 35(3)(b)**: DPIA required for large-scale special category data processing.

### EU AI Act
- **Art. 5(1)(c)**: Prohibition of AI systems that evaluate or classify persons based on social behaviour or personality characteristics leading to detrimental or unfavourable treatment.
- **Art. 10**: Data and data governance for high-risk AI — training data must be examined for bias (Art. 10(2)(f)).
- **Art. 10(5)**: Special category data processing for bias detection permitted with safeguards.
- **Art. 9(7)**: Risk management must address risks of bias and discrimination.

### EU Equality Directives
- **Directive 2000/43/EC**: Racial Equality Directive — prohibits discrimination based on racial or ethnic origin.
- **Directive 2000/78/EC**: Employment Equality Directive — prohibits discrimination based on religion, disability, age, sexual orientation.
- **Directive 2006/54/EC**: Gender Equality Directive — prohibits discrimination based on sex in employment.

## EDPB Guidance
- **EDPB Guidelines 04/2025**: AI systems that infer Art. 9 data from non-sensitive inputs process special category data.
- **EDPB Guidelines 06/2020**: Profiling that produces discriminatory effects on protected groups violates fairness principle.

## Technical Standards
- **ISO/IEC TR 24027:2021**: Bias in AI systems and AI-aided decision making — definitions, sources of bias, measurement approaches.
- **ISO/IEC 24028:2020**: AI trustworthiness — fairness as a trustworthiness dimension.
- **IEEE 7003-2021**: Algorithmic bias considerations — standard for addressing bias in algorithmic systems.
- **NIST SP 1270 (2022)**: Towards a Standard for Identifying and Managing Bias in AI — comprehensive framework for bias management.

## Enforcement Decisions
- **Dutch Court v. SyRI (ECLI:NL:RBDHA:2020:1878, 2020)**: Discriminatory algorithmic profiling struck down.
- **Italian DPA v. Deliveroo (2021)**: EUR 2.5M fine for discriminatory algorithmic management.
- **Austrian DPA v. AMS Algorithm (2020)**: Employment scoring algorithm found unlawful for scoring based on gender, age, disability.
- **Belgian Data Protection Authority v. Automated Decision-Making (2022)**: Investigation into discriminatory credit scoring.
