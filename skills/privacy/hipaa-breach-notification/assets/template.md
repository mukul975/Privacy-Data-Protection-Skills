We are writing to notify you of a security incident that may have involved your protected health information (PHI) maintained by the Stellar Payments Group Employee Health Plan.

## What Happened

On 13 March 2026, Stellar Payments Group discovered that an unauthorized third party gained access to systems containing employee health plan records between 10 March and 13 March 2026. The unauthorized access was the result of a targeted phishing attack that compromised an IT administrator's credentials. We immediately contained the incident, engaged a leading cybersecurity firm (Mandiant) to conduct a forensic investigation, and reported the matter to law enforcement.

Our investigation determined that the unauthorized person may have accessed files containing protected health information for approximately 8,420 current and former employees enrolled in the Stellar Payments Group Employee Health Plan.

## What Information Was Involved

The types of information that may have been accessed include:

- Full name
- Date of birth
- Social Security number
- Health plan member ID number
- Claims and enrollment information
- Diagnosis and procedure codes

Your specific records may not have included all of these data types.

## What We Are Doing

Upon discovering this incident, we took the following actions:

- Immediately contained the breach by isolating affected systems and revoking compromised credentials.
- Engaged Mandiant, a leading cybersecurity firm, to conduct a thorough forensic investigation.
- Reported the incident to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights.
- Notified law enforcement authorities.
- Implemented enhanced security measures including phishing-resistant multi-factor authentication, network segmentation, and enhanced monitoring.
