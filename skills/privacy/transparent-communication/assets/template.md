# Layered Privacy Notice — Layer 1 (Short Notice) Template

## How We Use Your Data

**Meridian Analytics Ltd** collects and uses your personal data to provide our data analytics services under your subscription agreement.

**What we collect:** Your name, contact details, payment information, and how you use our platform.

**Why:** To deliver our services, manage your account, process payments, and improve our platform.

**Legal basis:** Performance of our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b) UK GDPR) and, for analytics and improvement, our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) UK GDPR).

**Your rights:** You can access, correct, delete, or export your data at any time. You can also object to certain processing or restrict how we use your data.

**Contact our DPO:** dpo@meridiananalytics.co.uk

**Read our full privacy notice** for complete details on data sharing, international transfers, retention periods, and how to exercise your rights.
