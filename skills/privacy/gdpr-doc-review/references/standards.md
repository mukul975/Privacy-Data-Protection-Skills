# Standards and Regulatory References

## Primary Legislation

### GDPR Article 30 -- Records of Processing Activities
Mandatory documentation with specific field requirements for controllers (Art. 30(1)(a)-(g)) and processors (Art. 30(2)(a)-(d)).

### GDPR Articles 13-14 -- Transparency
Privacy notices must contain: controller identity, DPO contact, purposes, lawful basis, recipients, transfers, retention, rights, and automated decision-making information.

### GDPR Article 28(3) -- Processor Contracts
DPAs must contain all eight mandatory elements.

### GDPR Article 24 -- Controller Documentation
Controllers must maintain documentation demonstrating compliance.

## Regulatory Guidance and Enforcement

- **Romanian DPA (ANSPDCP, 2019)**: Fined Unicredit Bank EUR 130,000 for incomplete processing documentation.
- **ICO Documentation Guidance**: Documentation must be a living record reflecting current processing.

## ISO/IEC Standards

- **ISO/IEC 27701:2019**: Privacy Information Management System extending ISO 27001/27002 with privacy-specific controls aligned to GDPR.
- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022**: Information Security Management System providing security foundation for GDPR compliance.
