## 2. Scope of Joint Processing

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Processing Activities | Operation of the shared customer insights platform "InsightHub" which combines Nexus customer transaction data with Meridian analytical models to generate customer segmentation and purchasing propensity scores |
| Personal Data Categories | Customer name, email, purchase history, product preferences, website behaviour (pseudonymised), segmentation labels, propensity scores |
| Data Subject Categories | Nexus Technologies GmbH customers who have made at least one purchase and have active accounts (approximately 145,000 individuals) |
| Processing Location | EU data centres (Frankfurt, Germany — AWS eu-central-1) |

---

## 3. Purpose Determination

Both controllers jointly determine the purpose of processing as: generating customer segmentation insights and purchasing propensity scores to enable Nexus to personalise product recommendations and marketing communications, and to enable Meridian to improve and validate its analytical models.

- **Nexus's purpose**: Personalised marketing and product recommendations for existing customers based on data-driven segmentation.
- **Meridian's purpose**: Model validation and improvement using real-world transaction data to enhance the accuracy of predictive analytics products.

Both purposes are pursued through the common InsightHub platform and are interdependent.

## 8. Security Standards

Both parties commit to maintaining:
- ISO 27001 certified or equivalent information security management
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Role-based access control with quarterly access reviews
- Annual penetration testing by independent third parties
- Multi-factor authentication for all platform access
- Logging and monitoring of all data access with 12-month log retention

---

## 10. Liability

Both parties acknowledge joint and several liability to data subjects under Art. 82(4). Internally, liability is allocated proportionally to each party's contribution to the processing that caused the damage. Where a determination cannot be made, liability is shared equally. The party that pays full compensation to a data subject is entitled to claim contribution from the other party per Art. 82(5).

---

## 11. Term and Termination

- **Duration**: This arrangement is effective from 2026-02-01 and continues for an initial term of 24 months, renewable annually thereafter.
- **Termination**: Either party may terminate with 90 days' written notice. Upon termination, Meridian deletes all personal data received from Nexus within 30 days and provides written confirmation of deletion.
- **Survival**: Sections 9 (Breach Management), 10 (Liability), and security obligations survive termination for 3 years.

---

## 12. Art. 26(3) Acknowledgment

Both parties acknowledge that, irrespective of the terms of this arrangement, data subjects may exercise their rights under the GDPR against each and any of the joint controllers.
