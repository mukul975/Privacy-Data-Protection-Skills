## Findings

### Finding 1: Overdue New Hire Training (Critical)

- **Regulation**: 45 CFR §164.530(b)(2)(i)
- **Details**: 14 workforce members hired in Q4 2025 exceeded the 30-day training deadline before completing Tier 1 training. 3 of these individuals had active PHI access during the gap period.
- **Remediation**: Immediate training completion required. PHI access suspended for incomplete members. HR process updated to block PHI system provisioning until training confirmation received.
- **Deadline**: 2026-01-31
- **Owner**: HR Director + Privacy Officer

### Finding 2: Annual Refresher Non-Completion (Medium)

- **Regulation**: 45 CFR §164.308(a)(5)(i)
- **Details**: 71 workforce members (1.7%) did not complete annual refresher training by the December 31, 2025 deadline. Concentrated in Facilities/Support (19 members) and Volunteers/Trainees (14 members).
- **Remediation**: Escalation notices sent to department managers. Completion deadline extended to January 31, 2026 with weekly tracking.
- **Deadline**: 2026-01-31
- **Owner**: Department Managers

### Finding 3: Low Assessment Scores in Social Engineering Module (Medium)

- **Regulation**: 45 CFR §164.308(a)(5)(ii)(B)
- **Details**: Social engineering module had the lowest average score (86%) and pass rate (95.3%). 197 workforce members scored below 80% on first attempt.
- **Remediation**: Enhanced phishing simulation program implemented. Targeted retraining for workforce members who failed, including hands-on phishing email identification exercises.
- **Deadline**: 2026-02-28
- **Owner**: Security Officer

## Recommendations

1. Automate PHI access provisioning gate to require training completion confirmation before any system access is granted.
2. Increase phishing simulation frequency from quarterly to monthly given the lower social engineering assessment scores.
3. Implement mid-year refresher micro-training (10-minute modules) to supplement the annual comprehensive training.
4. Add department-level compliance dashboards visible to managers for real-time tracking.
