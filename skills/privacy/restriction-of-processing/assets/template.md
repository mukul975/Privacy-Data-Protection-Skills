Thank you for your request received on 28 February 2026 to restrict the processing of your personal data. We have assessed your request in accordance with Article 18 of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

We confirm that the ground for restriction under **Article 18(1)(a)** applies — you have contested the accuracy of specific personal data we hold about you, and we are in the process of verifying its accuracy.

Specifically, you have disputed the accuracy of the following data:

- Employer recorded as "Clearview Consulting Ltd" (you state the correct employer is "Clearview Advisory LLP")

## 2. Restriction Measures Applied

We have applied the following restriction measures effective 03 March 2026:

| Measure | Description | Status |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Database restriction flag | All records associated with your account have been flagged as restricted | Applied |
| Application query exclusion | Your data is excluded from all standard processing operations, reporting, and analytics | Applied |
| API access control | Any API requests returning your data will receive a restricted-data response | Applied |

| Access restriction | Access to your data is limited to the Data Protection Office and Legal team | Applied |

## 3. Processing During Restriction

While the restriction is in effect, your personal data will only be processed:
- With your specific consent for a particular processing activity
- For the establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims
- For the protection of the rights of another person
- For reasons of important public interest

Your data will continue to be stored securely but will not be used for any other purpose.
